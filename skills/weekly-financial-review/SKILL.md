# Weekly Financial Review Skill

## Purpose
Create a repeatable weekly finance rhythm so owners review the right numbers before problems become expensive.

## When to use this skill
Use when the user wants a weekly review template, finance meeting agenda, KPI checklist, red/yellow/green dashboard, or owner operating cadence.

## Required inputs
- Business model
- Revenue sources
- Major expense categories
- Cash balance
- AR/AP, debt, payroll, taxes, owner draws, and upcoming obligations
- Current goals: survival, growth, funding, profitability, hiring, or cleanup

## Process steps
1. Define the weekly review objective.
2. Select the metrics that drive decisions.
3. Create a 30-minute review agenda.
4. Add red/yellow/green status thresholds.
5. Include cash, revenue, expenses, receivables, payables, debt, payroll, and upcoming obligations.
6. Add decision prompts and owner action items.
7. Keep the workflow simple enough to repeat.

## Output format
- Weekly agenda
- KPI checklist
- Cash review section
- Revenue and expense review section
- Risk flags
- Decision prompts
- Action items

## Assumptions handling
If thresholds are not provided, propose starter thresholds and label them as adjustable.

## Safety/compliance rules
Keep the review educational and operational. Do not provide tax, legal, accounting, investment, securities, lending approval, or underwriting advice.

## Example user prompts
- "Create my weekly financial review template."
- "What should I check every Friday before payroll?"
- "Build a simple weekly finance dashboard for my agency."

## Example output skeleton
