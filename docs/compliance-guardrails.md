# Compliance Guardrails

CFO-in-a-Box provides business planning, financial visibility, operational analysis, and decision-support workflows. It does not provide regulated professional advice.

## Hard boundaries

CFO-in-a-Box must not provide:

- Tax advice
- Legal advice
- Certified accounting advice
- Audit, assurance, or CPA attestations
- Investment advice
- Securities advice
- Lending approval advice
- Underwriting decisions
- Guaranteed funding outcomes
- Debt settlement, bankruptcy, or insolvency advice

## Required positioning

Use language such as:

- Planning support
- Decision support
- Directional analysis
- Funding readiness
- Cash flow visibility
- Scenario modeling
- Preparation checklist
- Provider criteria apply

Avoid language such as:

- Guaranteed approval
- Preapproved unless a provider has explicitly issued it
- Lender-approved
- CPA-certified
- Tax strategy
- Legal recommendation
- Investment recommendation
- Underwriting decision

## Funding language

Funding readiness outputs must include this concept:

> This funding readiness score is not an approval, preapproval, underwriting decision, offer, commitment, or guarantee of financing. It is a planning tool to help identify strengths, weaknesses, and preparation gaps. Final decisions depend on providers, underwriters, investors, or capital partners and their criteria.

## General financial analysis disclaimer

Use when appropriate:

> This analysis is for business planning and financial decision support only. It is not tax, legal, accounting, investment, securities, underwriting, or lending advice. Confirm major decisions with the appropriate licensed professional.

## Data quality language

If financials are incomplete:

> This analysis is useful directionally, but it is not lender-grade yet because key information is missing or incomplete.

## Red flags requiring professional review

Recommend qualified professional review when users ask about:

- Tax filings, deductions, payroll taxes, or entity tax treatment
- Contract terms, employment law, legal exposure, or collections litigation
- Securities offerings, investor solicitation, valuations for securities, or cap table advice
- Formal CPA-reviewed statements, audit, assurance, or compiled financials
- Bankruptcy, insolvency, debt settlement, or creditor negotiations
- Loan approval decisions, underwriting outcomes, or provider-specific requirements not supplied by the provider

## Data privacy rules

Do not commit or expose:

- API keys, OAuth tokens, client secrets, or credentials
- Bank login credentials
- Full account numbers
- SSNs, EINs, driver license numbers, passport numbers
- Raw client financial documents
- Private payroll reports
- Tax returns
- Customer/vendor lists with private contact details

Use synthetic sample data for repo docs, schemas, templates, and evals.
