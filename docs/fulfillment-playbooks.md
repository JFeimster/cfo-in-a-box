# Fulfillment Playbooks

These playbooks define how CFO-in-a-Box workflows can be delivered across Free GPT, Starter Lab, Founder OS, and Advisor Desk tiers.

## Free GPT Playbook

### Goal
Give users immediate clarity and expose whether they need deeper support.

### Typical workflows
- Basic financial health check
- Burn rate and runway estimate
- Funding readiness self-check
- Weekly financial review template
- Plain-English P&L explanation

### Inputs
- User-provided numbers or uploads
- Current cash balance
- Revenue and expenses
- Business model and goal

### Output
- Directional analysis
- Assumptions and missing data
- Recommended next actions
- CTA to templates, Founder OS, or Advisor Desk when appropriate

## Starter Lab Playbook

### Goal
Give DIY owners templates, education, and repeatable operating systems.

### Deliverables
- Notion templates
- Google Sheets templates
- Prompt vault
- Weekly review checklist
- Cash flow forecast template
- Funding readiness checklist
- Cost leak worksheet

### Fulfillment rhythm
1. Member joins.
2. Member receives template library.
3. Member completes intake checklist.
4. Member runs weekly review.
5. Member attends cash flow clinic or AMA.

## Founder OS Playbook

### Goal
Install a practical financial operating system for owners who want monthly reporting and better decisions.

### Deliverables
- Intake workflow
- Financial data normalization
- Cash flow dashboard or template
- Monthly financial snapshot
- Funding readiness tracking
- Reminder and review cadence
- Export-based or lightweight integration workflow

### Fulfillment steps
1. Intake and goal definition
2. Document/data collection
3. Data normalization
4. Dashboard/template setup
5. First monthly snapshot
6. Review of risks, gaps, and next actions
7. Monthly reporting cadence

## Advisor Desk Playbook

### Goal
Add human-in-the-loop review and strategic support on top of Founder OS.

### Deliverables
- Monthly report review
- Advisor memo
- Monthly or biweekly review call
- Funding readiness support
- Hiring/spending/borrowing/cost-cutting decision support

### Fulfillment steps
1. Review Founder OS monthly snapshot.
2. Check assumptions and data quality.
3. Identify urgent risks.
4. Create advisor memo.
5. Prepare review call agenda.
6. Document owner decisions and follow-up actions.
7. Update next-month watchlist.

## Standard monthly cadence

| Week | Focus | Output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Week 1 | Close prior month and normalize data | Monthly snapshot draft |
| Week 2 | Review risks and cash forecast | Advisor memo or owner notes |
| Week 3 | Execute action plan | Cost cleanup, AR/AP follow-up, funding prep |
| Week 4 | Plan next month | Forecast updates and decision list |

## Quality checks

Before sending any report:

- Confirm period reviewed.
- Separate facts from assumptions.
- Check formulas.
- Mark missing data.
- Include compliance disclaimers when needed.
- End with prioritized actions.
- Remove sensitive data not needed for the report.
