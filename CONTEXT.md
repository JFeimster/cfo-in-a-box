# CFO-in-a-Box Context

CFO-in-a-Box is an AI CFO / FinanceOps platform for small business owners who need financial clarity without hiring a full-time CFO.

## Product promise

CFO-level clarity without CFO-level payroll.

## Core users

- Small business owners
- Founders and solopreneurs
- Agencies and consultants
- Ecommerce sellers
- Contractors and local service businesses
- Early-stage startups
- Operators preparing for funding conversations

## Core capabilities

- Financial statement analysis
- Cash flow forecasting
- Burn rate and runway calculation
- Funding readiness scoring
- Expense leak detection
- Scenario modeling
- Hiring affordability analysis
- Weekly financial review systems
- Monthly financial snapshots
- Advisor memo generation
- Founder OS / Advisor Desk workflows

## Default operating principle

Cash is the oxygen. Profit is the scoreboard. Forecasting is the radar.

## Default report structure

1. Executive Summary
2. Key Metrics
3. What the Numbers Mean
4. Risks
5. Opportunities
6. Recommended Actions
7. Assumptions and Missing Data

## Funding readiness framework

Score from 0 to 100 across:

1. Financial Documentation — 20 points
2. Cash Flow Health — 25 points
3. Profitability & Margins — 20 points
4. Debt & Repayment Capacity — 15 points
5. Growth Story & Use of Funds — 10 points
6. Owner / Business Risk Profile — 10 points

## Score interpretation

- 85–100: Strong funding candidate
- 70–84: Potentially fundable with cleanup
- 55–69: Needs financial strengthening before serious funding conversations
- 40–54: High-risk; fix cash flow, documentation, and profitability first
- 0–39: Not funding-ready; stabilize before pursuing capital

## Product ladder

### Free GPT
Basic financial checklist, burn/runway estimates, weekly review prompts, and funding readiness self-checks.

### Starter Lab
Templates, prompt vault, community, cash flow clinic, and DIY operating resources.

### Founder OS
FinOps setup, monthly reports, dashboards, alerts, funding readiness tracking, and export-based workflows.

### Advisor Desk
Human-in-the-loop review, advisor memos, review calls, funding preparation support, and decision support.

## Compliance posture

CFO-in-a-Box provides planning support and financial decision support. It does not provide tax, legal, accounting, investment, securities, underwriting, or lending approval advice.

Funding readiness outputs are not approvals, preapprovals, offers, commitments, underwriting decisions, or guarantees of financing.
